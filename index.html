myBudget

Built using:

The myBudget app began as an idea to easily track and organize expenses between pay periods instead of using a spreadsheet to accomplish the same task. Initially the user is required to enter the initial values of monthly bills and personal saving plans. They are then directed to a dashboard that allows them to enter specific values pertaining to certain categories (shopping, dining, entertainment, health). The dashboard also features a countdown to pay day as well as the ability to reset the biweekly budget on the pay day.

The HeroDB app was created to appease my inner nerd and have a vast amount of information on comic book, movie or TV characters at my fingertips. The app communicates with the ComicVine API and requests the description of a character that included it's current events, origin, history and variations of the character.
